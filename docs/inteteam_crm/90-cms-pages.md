# CMS Pages

Create and manage content pages for your website (Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, custom pages).

**CMS -> Pages**, or navigate to `/admin/cms-pages`

---

## Page List

A table showing all your pages:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|-------------|
| **Title** | Page title (click to view) |
| **Slug** | URL-friendly name |
| **Type** | Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, or Custom |
| **Status** | Published (green) or Draft (grey) |
| **Updated** | Last update date |

Use the **search box** and **type filter** to find pages.

---

## Creating a Page

1. Click **New Page**
2. Enter the title, slug, type, and content
3. Save as Draft or Publish immediately

---

## Managing Pages

From the **action dropdown** on each row:
- **View** — see the page as it appears
- **Edit** — change content
- **Publish / Unpublish** — toggle visibility
- **Delete** — remove the page
