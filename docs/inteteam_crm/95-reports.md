# Financial Reports

View financial summaries and export data.

**Business -> Reports**, or navigate to `/admin/reports`

---

## Date Range

Select a time period using the **preset buttons**:
- This month, Last month, This quarter, Last quarter, This year

Or set a **custom date range** using the From/To date inputs.

---

## Summary Cards

Four cards at the top showing the big picture:

| Card | What it shows |
|------|-------------|
| **Total Revenue** | All money received in the period |
| **Total Costs** | Parts, team, and stock costs |
| **Gross Profit** | Revenue minus costs |
| **Profit Margin** | Profit as a percentage |

---

## Breakdowns

### Revenue by Method

Table showing income by payment method (Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, Cheque, Dojo, Stripe, PayPal) with transaction counts and amounts.

### Costs Breakdown

Table showing costs by category: Parts, Team, and Stock (COGS).

### Invoices

Card showing: invoices issued, collected amount, outstanding amount, and VAT.

### Daily Revenue

Table with one row per day showing gateway revenue, manual revenue, and total — with a visual bar chart.

### Bookings

Card showing how many bookings were created and completed in the period.

---

## Exporting

Click **Export CSV** to download the report data as a spreadsheet.
