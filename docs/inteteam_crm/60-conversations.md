# Conversations

A unified inbox for all customer messaging — WhatsApp and SMS in one place.

**Conversations** in the top navigation, or navigate to `/admin/conversations`

---

## What You See

A chat interface showing all conversations across messaging channels. Messages from WhatsApp and SMS appear together so you don't need to switch between apps.

The left panel shows your conversation list, the right panel shows the selected conversation's messages.

**Requires:** WhatsApp and/or ClickSend SMS to be configured in Settings.
