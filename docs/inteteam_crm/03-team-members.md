# Managing Team Members

Add and manage the staff who use your CRM. Go to **Business menu -> Team -> All Users**, or navigate to `/admin/users`.

---

## Viewing Your Team

The team page shows a table with all users in your company:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | User's name (shows "(you)" next to your own) |
| **Email** | Their email address |
| **Role** | Colour-coded badge — Admin (purple), Team Member (orange), etc. |
| **Status** | Active (green), Pending (yellow), or Disabled (red) |

Use the **search box** to find users by name or email (type at least 3 characters).

---

## Inviting a New User

1. Click **Invite User** (top right)
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** — their full name
   - **Email** — their email address
   - **Role** — choose from the dropdown (see Roles below)
3. Click **Send Invitation**

The system sends an invitation email. The user has **7 days** to accept it. Until they do, their status shows as **Pending** (yellow).

Once they click the link in the email, their status changes to **Active** (green) and they can log in.

---

## Roles

| Role | Level | What they can do |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| **Team Member** | Basic | Work on assigned bookings only. Cannot see customer details or unassigned jobs |
| **View Only** | Basic | View inventory and warehouses, reserve stock. Cannot modify anything |
| **Full Access** | Mid | Edit items, subtract stock, move items between warehouses. Cannot add new stock |
| **Gallery Manager** | Mid | Manage galleries and media. Upload, organise, publish collections |
| **Warehouse Manager** | Senior | Full inventory control — add stock, create items, manage warehouses |
| **Admin** | Full | Everything above + user management + company settings |

Choose the role that matches what this person needs to do. You can change it later.

---

## Editing a User

1. Click the **three-dot menu** next to the user
2. Click **Edit**
3. Change their name, email, or role
4. Click **Save Changes**

---

## Disabling a User

If someone leaves or you need to temporarily revoke access:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** next to the user
2. Click **Disable**
3. Confirm

Their status changes to **Disabled** (red). They can no longer log in. Their data is preserved.

To re-enable: click the three-dot menu -> **Enable**.

---

## Removing a User

To permanently remove someone from your company:

1. Click the **three-dot menu** next to the user
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm

This removes them from your company only. If they belong to other companies in the system, those are unaffected.

---

## Viewing User Details

Click any user's name to see their detail page:

- **Profile card** — avatar, name, email, role, status
- **Timeline** — when they were invited, who invited them, when they accepted, and when they were disabled (if applicable)
