# Incoming Bookings

**Bookings -> Incoming** or navigate to `/admin/bookings/incoming`

This is your inbox for new repair jobs. Every booking starts here.

---

## What You See

A table of all bookings in the **Incoming** stage. Use the filters to narrow down:

- **Filter by status** — show only bookings with a specific status (your custom statuses)
- **Filter by team member** — show only bookings assigned to a specific person
- **Search** — search by booking ID, name, email, or phone

Each row shows the booking ID, customer, date, priority, status, and task progress.

---

## Processing an Incoming Booking

### 1. Review the Booking

Click the **Booking ID** to open the detail page. Check:

- Customer details (Customer tab)
- What was submitted via the booking form (Form Data section on Details tab)
- Any notes

### 2. Assign Team Members

On the booking detail page, **Details tab -> Assigned Team**:

1. Click the **assignee dropdown**
2. Select a team member
3. They'll appear in the assigned list
4. Repeat to assign multiple people

To remove someone, click the **X** next to their name.

### 3. Set Priority

On the booking detail page:

- **Customer priority** — how urgent the customer thinks it is
- **Internal priority** — your team's assessment (Low, Medium, High)

### 4. Add Tasks

Switch to the **Tasks tab**:

- Type a task and press Enter to add it
- Or click **Apply Template** to add a preset group of tasks (see [Booking Task Templates](15-booking-todos.md))
- Tick tasks off as they're completed
- Drag to reorder

### 5. Move to Undergoing

When you're ready to start work:

1. Click the **Move to Undergoing** button (top right of booking page, or from the row action menu in the list)
2. Select the **Internal Priority** (Low, Medium, High)
3. Click **Move to Undergoing**

The booking moves to the Undergoing list.
