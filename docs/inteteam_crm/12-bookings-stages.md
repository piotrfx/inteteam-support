# Moving Bookings Through Stages

## Moving Forward

### Incoming -> Undergoing

1. Open the booking or use the row action menu
2. Click **Move to Undergoing**
3. Select **Internal Priority** (Low, Medium, High)
4. Confirm

### Undergoing -> Completed

1. Open the booking or use the row action menu
2. Click **Complete**
3. The booking moves immediately to Completed

---

## Moving Back

Sometimes you need to return a booking to a previous stage.

### Undergoing -> Incoming

1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the booking (detail page or list row)
2. Click **Move Back**
3. Enter a **reason** for moving back (required)
4. Click **Move Back**

### Completed -> Undergoing

Same process — three-dot menu -> Move Back -> enter reason -> confirm.

The reason is recorded in the booking history so your team knows why it was moved.

---

## Cancelling a Booking

1. Click the **three-dot menu** on the booking
2. Click **Cancel Booking**
3. You'll see a warning with the booking reference
4. Enter a **reason for cancellation** (required, up to 500 characters)
5. Click **Cancel Booking** (red button)

Cancelled bookings are soft-deleted — they won't appear in your lists but the data is preserved.

---

## Changing Status (Within a Stage)

Each stage can have custom sub-statuses (e.g. "Waiting for parts", "Customer contacted"). These are the coloured badges you see on each booking.

To change a booking's status:

1. In the booking list, click the **coloured status badge** on any row
2. Select the new status from the dropdown
3. The change takes effect immediately

You can also change status from the booking detail page header.

To create custom statuses, see [Customizing Stage Statuses](14-booking-statuses.md).
