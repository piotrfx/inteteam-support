# Getting Started

## Logging In

1. Open your CRM URL in a browser (e.g. `https://crm.yourshop.com`)
2. You'll see the **Welcome back** login screen
3. Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**
4. Optionally tick **Remember me** to stay logged in
5. Click **Log in**

If your company uses InteTeam SSO, you'll see a **Sign in with Inte.Team SSO** button at the top — click that instead and you'll be redirected to the SSO portal.

### Forgot Your Password?

1. On the login screen, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your inbox for the reset email
5. Click the link in the email
6. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters) and confirm it
7. Click **Reset Password** — you'll be taken back to the login screen

### Two-Factor Authentication

If your admin has enabled 2FA, after entering your password you'll see a **Two-Factor Authentication** screen:

- **Authenticator app:** Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
- **Recovery code:** If you don't have your authenticator, click **Use a recovery code** and enter one of your backup codes

Tick **Remember this device for 30 days** if you don't want to enter a code every time on this device.

## The Dashboard

After logging in, you land on the **Dashboard**. This gives you a quick overview of your business:

### Stats Cards (top row)

| Card | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Warehouses** | Total number of warehouse locations |
| **Inventory Items** | Total items across all warehouses |
| **Low Stock Items** | Items below minimum stock level (highlighted in red) |
| **Team Members** | Number of staff in your company |

### Bookings Chart

Below the stats is a **Bookings — last 12 months** bar chart showing:

- **Blue bars** — Booked in (incoming)
- **Amber bars** — Undergoing repair
- **Green bars** — Completed

Hover over any month to see exact numbers. Use the toggle buttons below the chart to show/hide specific stages.

### Quick Actions

At the bottom you'll find quick links to:
- View Inventory
- Manage Warehouses
- Team Management

## Navigation

The top navigation bar is always visible. Here's what each section contains:

### Main Menu Items

| Menu | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Back to the main dashboard |
| **Scheduler** | Calendar view for appointments and visits |
| **Conversations** | Unified messenger for all customer communications |

### Dropdown Menus

**CMS** — Your website content:
- Forms, Pages, Updates, Gallery, Footer, About

**Bookings** — The core repair workflow:
- Incoming, Undergoing, Completed, Settings

**Business** — Everything else (two-column menu):
- *Left column:* Tasks, Invoices, Payments, Reports, Team
- *Right column:* Warehouse, Parts & Stock, Storefront

### Right Side Icons

| Icon | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **Barcode scanner** (barcode icon) | Opens the camera barcode scanner — scan a label to jump to that booking/item |
| **Notification bell** | Shows unread notifications |
| **Your avatar** | Profile settings, theme, logout |

### On Mobile

On phones and tablets, the menu collapses into a **hamburger menu** (three lines icon) on the left. Tap it to open the full navigation. The barcode scanner, notification bell, and user menu remain visible at all times.
