# Managing Your Profile

View and update your contact details.

**Navigation:** Click **Profile** in the top menu.

---

## What You See

| Field | Editable? | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Email** | No | Your login email — this cannot be changed |
| **Name** | Yes | Your display name shown on bookings and orders |
| **Phone** | Yes | Your phone number — used for contact by the shop |

---

## Updating Your Details

1. Change the **Name** or **Phone** field
2. Click **Save**
3. You'll see a confirmation message: "Profile updated."

### Tips

- Your email is tied to your login and cannot be changed from the portal
- If you need to change your email, contact the shop directly
- Use an international format for your phone number (e.g. +44 7912 345678)
