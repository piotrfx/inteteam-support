# Getting Started

The customer portal lets you check on your repairs, view your orders, and manage your contact details — all in one place.

---

## Accessing the Portal

Open the portal link provided by your shop. This is usually something like `portal.yourshop.com` or `yourshop.portal.inte.team`.

---

## Logging In

The portal uses a verification code sent to your email — no password needed.

1. Enter your **Email address** and click **Send verification code**
2. Check your inbox for an email from your shop with a 6-digit code
3. Enter the code on the portal
4. Click **Verify**

You're now logged in and will see your dashboard.

### Tips

- The code expires after **10 minutes** — if it doesn't work, click **Use a different email** and request a new one
- If you don't see the email, check your spam folder
- You can paste the code directly — it will auto-fill all 6 digits

### First Time?

If this is your first time logging in, the portal will automatically create your account using your email address. Any bookings or orders linked to that email will appear on your dashboard straight away.

---

## Logging Out

1. Click the **log out** icon in the top navigation bar (desktop: top-right, mobile: in the menu)
2. You'll be returned to the login page

---

## Navigation

After logging in, the top navigation bar shows:

| Menu Item | What It Shows |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of your recent bookings and orders |
| **Bookings** | All your repair bookings with status |
| **Orders** | All your shop orders with payment status |
| **Profile** | Your contact details |

On mobile, tap the **menu icon** (three lines) to open the navigation.
