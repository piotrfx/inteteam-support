# Dashboard

The dashboard is your home page after logging in. It shows a summary of your recent activity.

**Navigation:** Click **Dashboard** in the top menu, or log in to land here automatically.

---

## What You See

### Welcome Greeting

At the top, you'll see a personalised greeting with your name (if you've set one in your profile).

### Recent Bookings

A list of your most recent repair bookings, showing:

| Column | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Reference** | Your booking reference number (e.g. REF-A7B3X9) |
| **Device/Issue** | What device or problem was reported |
| **Stage** | Current status badge — Incoming, Undergoing, or Completed |
| **Date** | When the booking was created |

Click any booking to see its full details.

Click **View all** to go to the full bookings list.

### Recent Orders

A list of your most recent shop orders, showing:

| Column | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Reference** | Order reference number |
| **Total** | Order amount in GBP |
| **Status** | Payment status (Paid, Pending, etc.) |
| **Date** | When the order was placed |

Click any order to see its full details.

Click **View all** to go to the full orders list.

---

## No Bookings or Orders?

If you're new or haven't made any bookings yet, you'll see a message saying "No bookings yet" or "No orders yet". These sections will fill in as you interact with the shop.
