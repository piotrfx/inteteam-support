# Customer Portal — User Manual Scope

**App:** store_front (Customer Portal)
**Audience:** End-customers of tenant shops
**Access:** `portal.inte.team` or custom domain per tenant

## What This Covers

Guides for end-customers who use the self-service portal to check bookings, view orders, and manage their profile. These customers are NOT staff — they're the people who bring their devices in for repair or buy products.

## Roles

| Role | Access | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Guest** | Login page only | Anyone visiting the portal before signing in |
| **Customer** | Full portal | Authenticated customer (logged in via OTP) |
