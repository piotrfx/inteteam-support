# Dark Mode & Appearance

Switch between light and dark mode for comfortable viewing.

---

## Toggling Dark Mode

1. Click the **sun/moon icon** in the top navigation bar
2. The portal switches immediately between light and dark mode

Your choice is saved automatically — it will stay the same when you come back.

---

## How It Looks

| Mode | Background | Text |
|------|-----------|------|
| **Light** | White/light grey | Dark text |
| **Dark** | Dark grey/black | Light text |

The shop's brand colours (buttons, links, accents) stay consistent in both modes.

> **Note:** If the shop has disabled dark mode, you won't see the toggle icon.
