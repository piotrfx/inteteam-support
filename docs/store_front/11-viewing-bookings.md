# Viewing Bookings

View all your repair bookings and track their progress through each stage.

**Navigation:** Click **Bookings** in the top menu.

---

## The Bookings List

Your bookings are displayed as cards, each showing:

| Field | What It Shows |
|-------|---------------|
| **Reference** | Your booking reference (e.g. INT-001/03-2026) |
| **Device/Issue** | The device type or problem description |
| **Stage** | Coloured badge showing current status |
| **Date** | When the booking was created |

### Booking Stages

Every repair goes through three stages:

| Stage | Badge Colour | Meaning |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Incoming** | Blue | Your booking has been received and is waiting to be picked up |
| **Undergoing** | Amber | Your device is being repaired |
| **Completed** | Green | The repair is finished and ready for collection |

### Filtering by Stage

Use the tabs at the top of the list to filter:

- **All** — show everything
- **Incoming** — only new bookings
- **Undergoing** — only bookings being repaired
- **Completed** — only finished bookings

### Pagination

If you have many bookings, use the **Previous** and **Next** buttons at the bottom to move between pages.

---

## Booking Details

Click any booking card to see the full details:

### What You See

- **Reference** — your booking reference (e.g. INT-001/03-2026)
- **Stage** — current status badge
- **Device** — what device was booked in
- **Issue** — the problem that was reported
- **Location** — which shop location is handling the repair
- **Created** — when the booking was made

### Scheduled Visits

If visits have been scheduled for your booking (e.g. a drop-off or collection appointment), you'll see them listed with:

- **Date and time** of the appointment
- **Status** of the visit

### Going Back

Click **Back to bookings** at the top to return to the list.
