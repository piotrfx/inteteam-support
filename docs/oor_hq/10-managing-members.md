# Managing VIP Members

Company admins can control which family members have VIP access.

**VIP Settings** in OOR-HQ, or navigate to the VIP settings page.

---

## Enabling VIP Access

1. Go to **VIP Settings**
2. Find the family member in the list
3. Click **Enable**
4. They can now use "Enter VIP" on their dashboard

---

## Disabling VIP Access

1. Go to **VIP Settings**
2. Find the family member
3. Click **Disable**
4. They can no longer start VIP sessions
5. If they have an active session, it will be stopped

---

## Who Can Manage Members

Only **company admins** can enable or disable VIP access for members. Regular family members cannot change their own VIP status.
