# VIP Overview

## What is VIP?

VIP (Vintage Internet Project) lets you browse the modern web using a streamed browser session running on our server. The session is rendered server-side and streamed to your device — even vintage hardware like Windows 95/98 machines can access modern websites.

Each company gets its own isolated container. Your browsing is private and separated from other families.

---

## How It Works

1. Log into OOR-HQ
2. Click **Enter VIP** on your dashboard
3. The system starts your container (if it's not already running)
4. You're redirected to your personal VIP URL (e.g. `yourfamily.vip.domain.com`)
5. A full Chromium browser appears — browse any website
6. The system tracks your usage in minutes per month

You don't need a separate login for the VIP session — it uses single sign-on from OOR-HQ.

---

## Session Rules

- **Idle timeout:** If you stop using the session for 5 minutes, you'll get a warning
- **Grace period:** You have 5 more minutes to click "Keep session alive" before the session stops
- **Monthly limit:** Your company has an allocated number of VIP minutes per month
- **Final hour warning:** When you have approximately 1 hour of VIP time left this month, you'll be notified
- **Reconnect:** If your browser tab closes, you can click "Enter VIP" again to reconnect to the same session (your tabs are preserved)

---

## What You Need

- An OOR-HQ account with VIP access enabled by your company admin
- Any web browser (modern or vintage)
- Internet connection
